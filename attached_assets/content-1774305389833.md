## Frequently Asked Questions

What is mobile IV therapy and how does it work?

Mobile IV therapy is a convenient service that delivers drip treatments in your home, office, or event in select cities. The therapy is administered by registered nurses supervised by a medical doctor. Therapy involves infusing a saline solution enriched with vitamins, minerals and/or medication directly into the bloodstream for fast absorption.

Can you get IV therapy at home?

Yes. Mobile IV therapy can be scheduled almost anywhere including your home. Our registered nurses will provide all the materials required so all you need to do is sit back and relax.

What are the benefits of getting IV therapy at home?

Receiving IV therapy at home is not only convenient for your schedule but can be the most comfortable setting. With home therapy, you don’t need to travel to a medical office, helpful for those with mobility issues, or spend time in a waiting room. You can even read, watch TV or work during therapy.


What conditions or symptoms can mobile IV therapy help with?

IV drip therapy can help improve overall health and wellbeing, boost energy levels, recover from illnesses more quickly, and address a variety of specific health targets and goals.

How quickly can a mobile IV nurse come to my location?

In general, we can have a nurse to your home or office within 2 hours of booking in any of our coverage areas. We recommend booking an appointment in advance of a group event to ensure that you receive your desired time slot.


Is mobile IV therapy safe?

Mobile IV therapy is safe when administered by registered nurses, nurse practitioners or paramedics. These trained professionals follow strict guidelines on sterile equipment and proper protocols and screen a patient’s health conditions prior to administering therapy.

How long does a typical mobile IV therapy session take?

Most mobile IV therapy sessions typically last between 30 – 45 minutes depending on the individual. Our NAD+ and NAD+ Boost IVs take 1 – 2 hours per session to administer.


What types of IV treatments are available for in-home service?

Our vitamin IV treatments offer therapy addressing a range of symptoms and conditions from general wellness, recovery, and beauty. We also offer special IV treatments that include NAD+, Niagen NR, Iron, and Ketamine treatments.


Do I need a prescription or doctor’s approval for mobile IV therapy?

A prescription or doctor approval is not required for mobile IV therapy. After an initial online medical screening, which is reviewed by a medical doctor, you can receive mobile IV therapy.


How much does mobile IV therapy cost?

Our mobile IV therapy treatments start at $249 (Dehydration) which includes 1L of IV fluids and electrolytes, and range up to $399 which is our most premium IV blend (All-Inclusive). Prices may vary depending on location. Our NAD+ IV Boost ($999) is administered over several sessions and can address more in-depth health goals such as anti-aging and exercise performance enhancement.

Does insurance cover IV therapy?

Typically, insurance does not cover IV therapy treatments. In some situations, such as a medically diagnosed condition, insurance coverage may apply. Drip Hydration does not accept insurance and provides mobile IV therapy at reasonable prices.

Can I book mobile IV therapy for a group or event?

Yes. Mobile IV therapy for a group or group event. Our on-site event services include a registered nurse who can administer wellness and beauty IV therapy at corporate events, weddings, VIP events, and other types of large-scale events.
